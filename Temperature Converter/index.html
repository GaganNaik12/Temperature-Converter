Temperature Formulas

  • C → F: (°C × 9/5) + 32
  • F → C: (°F − 32) × 5/9
  • C → K: °C + 273.15
  • K → C: K − 273.15
  • F → K: (°F − 32) × 5/9 + 273.15
  • K → F: (K − 273.15) × 9/5 + 32

🌡️ Quick Facts

  • Absolute Zero = 0 K = −273.15 °C
  • Water Freezes = 0 °C = 32 °F = 273.15 K
  • Water Boils = 100 °C = 212 °F = 373.15 K
